About Us

Back in April 2021, current founder Callum Thompson was spending his afternoon with a fresh cup of coffee. He was tired of seeing the same, unimaginative themes throughout websites. So he decided to start his own independant company - Hot Beans Web - where we thrive on creativity and creating something new.
